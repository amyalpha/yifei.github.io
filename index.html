About Me

I am a sociologist and lecturer at Southwestern University of Finance and Economics, specializing in social stratification, social networks, inequality, and demographic change. My research explores the intersections of social capital, gender, and economic disparities in China and internationally.

My recent work includes studies on education policy, AI and digital inequality, and the impact of social networks on life chances. I am passionate about applying quantitative methods to address real-world questions in sociology.
