About Me

Hi, welcome to my personal page! I am Yifei Lu, a sociologist and Assistant Professor at the Southwestern University of Finance and Economics (SWUFE) in Chengdu, China. I earned my Ph.D. in Sociology from Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona in 2021, along with an M.Sc. in Quantitative Economics from Université Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne and an M.A. in Public Policy and Management from Korea Development Institute. My research focuses on social stratification, educational inequality, AI policy, and social and cultural capital. In my free time, I enjoy traveling, exploring gourmet cuisine, and diving into sci-fi, manga, and anime.
